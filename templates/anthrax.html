Anthrax

Symptoms

  • Sudden death
  • High fever
  • Blood-stained discharges
  • Rapidly decaying carcass
  • Shivering or twitching
  • Harsh dry cough
  • Blood in dung or in nostrils
  • Decrease or complete loss of milk
  • Fits
  • Bright staring eyes
  • Colicky pains
  • Dejection and loss of appetite
