Milk Fever (Cattle)

Symptoms

  • Loss of appetite
  • Lethargy
  • Dullness
  • Nervousness
  • Weight shifting
  • Dull and listless
  • Cold ears and a dry nose
  • Incoordination with walking
  • Muscle trembling

Cure

  • Milk Fever

    Preventive Measures

    • Ensure proper diet during late pregnancy
    • Balanced calcium and phosphorus levels in feed

    Chemical Measures

    • Intravenous calcium gluconate injection
    • Vitamin D supplements

    Biological Measures

    • Calcium supplementation through feed additives

    Indigenous Method

    • Use of jaggery and calcium-rich herbs in feed
