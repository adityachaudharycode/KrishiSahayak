Foot and Mouth Disease

Symptoms

  • Slobbering and smacking lips.
  • Shivering.
  • Tender and sore feet.
  • Reduced milk yield.
  • Sores and blisters on feet
  • Raised temperature

Cure

  • FMD (Foot-and-Mouth Disease)

    Preventive Measures

    • Isolation of sick animals
    • Sick animals should be attended by other persons
    • Cleaning and disinfection of shed
    • Proper disposal of dead animals
    • Maintaining vaccination schedule with FMD vaccine at 1.5 month, 4.5 month then 6 month interval

    Chemical Measures

    • Wash with KMnO4, apply boric powder on wound
    • Antibiotic and Fluid therapy may be provided

    Biological Measures

    • Fly repellent or Fly killer may be used inside or outside the cattle shed

    Indigenous Method

    • It is suggested to apply borax with honey
