Bird Flu

Symptoms

  • Sneezing
  • Coughing
  • Nasal discharge
  • Swollen infraorbital sinuses
  • Diarrhea
  • Vomiting
  • Abdominal pain
  • Swelling around the head, neck, and eyes
  • Purple discoloration
  • Tremors
  • Unusual postures
  • Problems with coordination
  • Egg production: Decreased
  • Ruffled feathers
  • Quietness
  • Depression
  • Drinking less water
  • Sudden, unexplained death
