Goat Pox

Symptoms

  • Red spots that develop into blisters
  • Nodules
  • Pustules
  • Thick crusts on the face, eyelids, ears, udder, and base of the tail
  • Fever (40–42°C)
  • Breathing difficulties caused by internal lesions
  • Eye and nose discharge
  • Loss of appetite
  • Lethargy and depression
