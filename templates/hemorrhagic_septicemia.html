Hemorrhagic Septicemia (HS)

Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Dullness
  • Reluctance to move
  • Salivation
  • Serous nasal discharge
  • Oedematous swellings in the pharyngeal region
  • Respiratory distress
  • Sudden death or a protracted course up to 5 days

Cure

  • HS (Hemorrhagic Septicemia)

    Preventive Measures

    • Regular vaccination programme with HS vaccine at 6 months then yearly
    • Sick animals treated properly
    • Disinfection of shed
    • Disposal of waste
    • Proper disposal of carcass

    Chemical Measures

    • Oxytetracycline
    • Trimethoprim/sulfamethoxazole
    • A combination of penicillin and streptomycin

    Biological Measures

    • Hot fomentation with salt pack may be given
    • Antitoxins may be used to treat affected animals

    Indigenous Method

    • NA
